mrrr (mûr)
interj.
  1. Used to greet someone
    • Matt opened the front door, and Chris said "Mrrr!"
  2. Used as a curse or comeback
    • Chris said, "Matt, that was a stupid move," and Matt replied, "well, mrrr!"
  3. Used as an incoherent uttering
    • "Matt, did I wake you up?" "Mrrr..." "Mrrr?" "Mrrr..."
  4. Used as a response when surprised or excited
    • Matt said, "hey, Resident Evil: The Movie just came out!" Chris exclaimed, "mrrr!"
mrrrness (mûr'nîs)
n.
  1. The quality or state of being mrrrlike
    • "That had lots of mrrrness."
mrrr.net (mûr'dðt-nêt)
n.
  1. The greatest web comic on the Internet. Ever.
    • "Hey, have you seen what mrrr.net posted today?"
[New English, from Norwegian mrrrph.]
